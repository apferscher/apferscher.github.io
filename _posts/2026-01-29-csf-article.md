Our article ["Incremental Fingerprinting in an Open World"](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.21680) has been accepted at the [39th IEEE Computer Security Foundations Symposium](https://csf2026.ieee-security.org/).

In this article, co-authored by Paul Kobialka, Einar Broch Johnsen, Sebastian Junges, and Jurriaan Rot, we propose a technique for fingerprinting black-box implementations, such as of communication protocols, in an open-world scenario. For this, we combine automata learning techniques with conformance checking techniques.
