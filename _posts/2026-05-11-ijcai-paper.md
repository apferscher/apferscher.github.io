Our paper Attribution-based Explanations for Markov Decision Processes has been accepted at the 35<sup>th</sup> International Joint Conference on Artificial Intelligence [(IJCAI)](https://2026.ijcai.org/) in Bremen, Germany.

In this paper, co-authored with Paul Kobialka, Francesco Leofante, Erika Ábráham, Einar Broch Johnsen and S. Lizeth Tapia Tarifa, we propose a technique for attribution-based explanations for Markov decision processes. This enables the attribution of importance in a sequential decision-making setting.
